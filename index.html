How OFFGRID works

The anti-algorithm idea. Engagement-optimised feeds reinforce what you already play. OFFGRID deliberately sources from orthogonal signals so you escape the bubble:

  • Cross-platform collaborative filtering ÔÇö Last.fm "people who like X also like Y", built on different data than video feeds.
  • Label-mate digging ÔÇö other artists on labels you rate (Discogs, optional).
  • Adjacent-subgenre exploration ÔÇö one step sideways from your core genres.
  • Lateral picks ÔÇö Claude proposes tastemaker deep cuts that pure similarity misses.

Then it filters for DJs: only your tempo window (with ┬Ż/├Ś2 matching) and harmonically-compatible Camelot keys survive; anything already in your crate is dropped.

Three tiers. Offline (always on): full taste analysis + smart search links. + Last.fm key: real similar-track discovery with match scores. + Anthropic key: Claude re-ranks, estimates BPM/key, and writes a one-line reason for every pick ÔÇö and grounds itself against the real candidate pool to avoid hallucinated tracks.
